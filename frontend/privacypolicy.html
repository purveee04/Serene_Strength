Serene Strength Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Welcome to Serene Strength! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights concerning that information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide and improve your personalized fitness and wellness service:

  • Account Data: Your name, email address, and encrypted password used for registration and login.
  • Fitness & Wellness Data: Information you log, such as workout history, yoga sessions completed, physical activity metrics, and general fitness goals.
  • AI Preference Data: Your responses to questionnaires and feedback used by our AI to create your personalized plans.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including class views, feature usage, and technical data (IP address, browser type).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to enhance your experience and deliver the Serene Strength service:

  • Personalized Coaching: To generate tailored workout routines, suggested yoga classes, and motivational tips based on your goals.
  • Service Improvement: To understand user behavior, identify potential app issues, and continuously improve our AI algorithms and content.
  • Communication: To send you essential updates about your account, billing, and (if enabled) **Notification** alerts about your personalized coaching.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party vendors (like payment processors or cloud hosting services) who assist us in operating the service, provided they adhere to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • Anonymized Data: We share aggregated, non-personally identifiable data for research or public reports. This data cannot be traced back to you.

4. Your Privacy Choices

You maintain control over your data, which you can manage easily in your **Settings** section:

  • Account Visibility: You can set your account to **Private** to prevent others from seeing your activity.
  • Notifications: You can opt-in or out of specific alerts like **Workout Session Reminders** and **Personalized AI Coaching Tips**.
  • Data Access & Deletion: You can request a copy of your data or permanently remove your account and associated data using the **Delete Account** option in Settings.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@serenestrength.com.
